A single delayed container
Hundreds of thousands in lost opening revenue and emergency air freight
A failed QC inspection at destination

A supplier that disappears mid-project
Everything. There is no recovery from a production partner who cannot deliver.

WHAT WE DO ABOUT IT

We structured our entire process to make these failures impossible.

Direct manufacturing oversight. Our team is on the ground across our production network in Java and Bali — long-term factory relationships built over two decades. We are not sourcing remotely. We are standing on the floor watching your project come together.

End-to-end project management. From design support and technical drawings through manufacturing, on-site QC, containerisation, shipping, and multi-destination delivery — one team, one point of accountability across the entire chain.

Quality control before it leaves the country. Every piece is inspected on the ground before export. Every container is documented. Problems are caught and resolved in production — not discovered at destination.

Two decades of doing this. 150+ hotels across 30 countries and six continents. The brands that return to us project after project — Four Seasons, Mandarin Oriental, Capella, Park Hyatt — do so because they stopped worrying.

Our clients are not buying furniture. They are buying the certainty that it will arrive right, on time, and exactly as specified.

FOUR SEASONS HOTELS & RESORTS

Safari Lodge Serengeti

Serengeti National Park, Tanzania
Interior Design Wilson Associates
Scale 77 Keys — Suites, Villas & Public Areas
THE PROJECT

One of the most logistically demanding deliveries in iBal's portfolio. The Serengeti location required overland transportation through Tanzania to a remote site with extreme environmental conditions. Every piece was engineered for durability in high-humidity, high-UV exposure while maintaining the refined aesthetic that Four Seasons demands. Custom prototyping ran through three full iterations before production sign-off.

Scope of Supply Case Goods, Upholstery, Outdoor Furniture, Decorative Lighting, Bathroom Accessories
Lead Time 14 months — Design to Final Delivery
Logistics Sea freight to Dar es Salaam, overland to Serengeti — phased delivery across 3 shipments
Sustainability FSC-certified timbers, locally inspired weaving techniques, low-VOC finishes throughout
MATERIALS & FINISHES
Solid Teak Hand-Woven Rattan Aged Brass Hardware Natural Linen Handcrafted Leather Local Sandstone Burnished Bronze Outdoor-Grade Teak
THE OUTCOME

Full FF&E package delivered on schedule across three phased shipments. Zero replacement claims at 12-month post-installation review. The lodge has since become one of Four Seasons' most photographed properties, with iBal furniture featured prominently in international hospitality and travel publications.

PARK HYATT

Jakarta

Jakarta, Indonesia
Interior Design Yabu Pushelberg
Scale 220 Keys — Rooms, Suites, Presidential Suite & Public Areas
THE PROJECT

iBal's largest single-property commission by room count. The Park Hyatt brand standard required millimetre-level precision across 220 rooms with six distinct room types, each with bespoke case goods and upholstery specifications. Prototyping alone involved 14 separate mock-up rooms reviewed by the design team before production approval. The project ran concurrently with building construction, requiring close coordination with the GC on installation sequencing.

Scope of Supply Case Goods, Upholstery, Banquette Seating, Decorative Screens, Custom Joinery, Bathroom Vanities
Lead Time 18 months — Prototyping through Final Installation
Logistics Domestic delivery from Bali production facility — coordinated floor-by-floor installation
Sustainability Reclaimed teak elements, water-based lacquers, Indonesian artisan partnerships
MATERIALS & FINISHES
American Walnut Black Anodised Aluminium Brushed Brass Italian Marble Hand-Stitched Leather Smoked Glass Custom Lacquer Woven Abaca
THE OUTCOME

Full FF&E delivered and installed ahead of the hotel's soft opening. The domestic logistics advantage — manufacturing in Bali for a Jakarta installation — reduced lead time by approximately three months compared to international sourcing. Hyatt's regional procurement team subsequently engaged iBal for additional pipeline projects in Southeast Asia.

Bangkok, Thailand

JUMEIRAH GROUP

Vittaveli

Maldives
Interior Design Hirsch Bedner Associates (HBA)
Scale 89 Keys — Beach Villas, Water Villas, Restaurants & Overwater Spa
THE PROJECT

The Maldives environment is among the most punishing for furniture — salt air, extreme UV, tropical humidity, and sand abrasion. Every specification decision on this project prioritised longevity without compromising the barefoot luxury aesthetic. The overwater villas required furniture engineered for marine conditions, including stainless steel internal hardware and marine-grade teak throughout. Logistics required containerised sea freight to Malé followed by barge transfer to the island, with all pieces individually wrapped for salt spray protection during transit.

Scope of Supply Case Goods, Outdoor Furniture, Pool Deck Furniture, Spa Treatment Furniture, Restaurant Seating
Lead Time 12 months — Design to Completion
Logistics Sea freight Bali to Malé — barge transfer to island atoll, containerised for salt protection
Sustainability Marine-grade coatings, UV-stabilised outdoor fabrics, coral-safe packaging protocols
MATERIALS & FINISHES
Marine-Grade Teak Powder-Coated Aluminium Sunbrella Fabrics Natural Coral Stone Stainless Steel Hardware Woven Synthetic Rattan Coconut Shell Inlay Weathered Driftwood Finish
THE OUTCOME

Complete delivery achieved within the resort's refurbishment window, minimising revenue impact from room closures. The outdoor collections have proven exceptional durability through two monsoon seasons without degradation. Jumeirah subsequently engaged iBal for additional resort properties in the group's pipeline.
